Click-Through Treatment Algorithm

Printable at any step. Designed for mobile — each positive-screen step links forward to the next without going back to the menu.

1

Screen at every health supervision visit (ages ≥ 10)

Ask: "Have you ever tried smoking, vaping, nicotine pouches, or chew?"

AAP screening script EHR template (.dot phrase) 🖨 Print screening questions
2A

Positive screen → Assess

  • Product type (vape pod, pouch, cigarette, chew)
  • Frequency & nicotine strength
  • Readiness to quit (motivational interview)
  • Co-use (cannabis, alcohol)
⭐ Penn State Dependence Index (recommended) Vape/pouch → NRT translator MI prompts cheat sheet
Continue: Go to Step 3 — age & consent ▶
2B

Negative screen → Prevent

Brief positive reinforcement + counsel on vape/pouch risks. Document & re-screen next visit.

🖨 Print prevention talking points
3

Determine pathway by age & consent

Under 14

Parental consent required for most pharmacotherapy. Lean on counseling + family-based intervention.

Family conversation guide Continue to treatment ▶

14 – 17

Behavioral first; off-label NRT per AAP if moderate–severe addiction + motivated. Parental consent generally required in ND.

AAP off-label NRT evidence Continue to treatment ▶

18 – 21

Full NRT, varenicline, bupropion eligible. No parental consent. Refer NDQuits.

Adult Rx quick-reference Continue to treatment ▶
4

Treat — Choose intervention

Pharmacotherapy

Dose using the simplified Penn State Index, or match to daily nicotine intake.
